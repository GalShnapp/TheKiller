Running the app

  2. Enter:" node ex1/server.js"
  3. From a web browser, go to "127.0.0.1:8081"

Using the app

New User

Upon visiting your local host, you will be transfered to the app's login/register page. Click the "Create an accout" button, create an account and the app wil redirect you to the main page.

Everyone

If you have not visted the webiste in the last 30 minutes, you'll be requested to login. Otherwise, feel free to use the idea board.

Development process

Looking back, to me, it seems natural divide the development process of this excersice to 2 phases. The first consists of designing the app, while the second phase dealt with implementing the user management system and scailing up the routing system.

Design

So, this part was not easy. I got to thinking about how I'm not happy with the design of my last submission and decided I want to re-do it. I found a design for a login/register page that looked good and used it. I ran into some trouble when trying to understand how to use their style inorder to create my app's maing page. Eventualy I've managed to use it, but I don't think it's too good of a fit for the purpse of this app and I inted to reset it once more for the next submission. I did end up having a lot of fun; and honestly, once I saw something pretty, It got me quite motivated to keep going and do something really cool up ahead.

Scailing

Much like it's predecessor; this phase wasn't a small bundle of joy either. I think the major hardship about this part was that I did not understand what was I doing well enough. The gap between "this is working" and "I understand this tool and am able to get it to do what ever I want it to" is quite large. Additionaly, I had a hard time designing a "rich" architechture. That is; There were a lot of decisions to be made, where I was unaware of the fact that there's a decision to make, let alone what might the right choice be, or what are the implications of eachi desicion.
